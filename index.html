How to Request Account Deletion - TensorArk

Account Deletion Process

To request the deletion of your TensorArk account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to hr@tensorark.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. In the email body, include:
    • Your registered email address
    • A statement requesting account deletion
Sample Email Template:
To: hr@tensorark.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request

I would like to request the deletion of my TensorArk account.

My registered email address: [Your Email Address]

Please delete all data associated with my account.

What Happens Next

  • We will process your request within 2 business days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once your account is deleted
  • For security purposes, we may request additional verification of your identity

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon processing your deletion request, we will remove:

  • Your profile information (name, email, etc.)
  • Your voice cloning history and associated files
  • Your authentication data
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Data Retention Information

Please note:

  • All personal data will be deleted from our active systems upon request processing
  • Backup systems may retain data for up to 30 days for system integrity purposes

Additional Information

For any questions about the account deletion process or data retention policies, please contact us at hr@tensorark.com

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All associated data will be permanently removed from our systems.
